Practice Areas

Personal Injury

Mr. Phillips has years of experience helping clients receive fair compensation for injuries caused by the negligent and wrongful actions of others. Our starting point is always you, the client.

Real Estate Law

Are you or your business in the process of buying or selling real estate? Do you have questions or concerns about contracts, sales or lease agreements, titles, easements, or liens?

Civil Litigation

Are you facing a legal issue that can be defined as civil in nature? Are you seeking monetary damages in a non-criminal dispute involving personal injury, real estate, employment, workers’ comp, or property damage?

Business Law

Are you considering forming a business? Do you know which type of business structure best suits your entity? Choosing the right legal structure for an enterprise is a critical step with major tax, liability, and growth implications.

Criminal Law Defense

Have you been charged with a criminal offense, revocation of your probation, or are presently released on bond? Regardless of the plea, American citizens enjoy the presumption of innocence and the right to an attorney.
